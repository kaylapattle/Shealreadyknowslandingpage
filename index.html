questions

Before you start.

is this therapy?

No. She Already Knows isn't a replacement for therapy or medical care. It's a daily practice for decision-making and self-trust — think of it as a mirror, not a clinician.

how is this different from chatgpt?

ChatGPT can answer literally anything — that's kind of the problem. She Already Knows only does one thing: reflect your own thinking back to you, in a style built specifically around self-trust and self-abandonment patterns, not generic advice. No more spending hours prompt-crafting to get something useful out of it — you just say what you're stuck on.

what happens after my free response?

You get one reflection completely free, no account needed. If you want to keep going, you'll create an account — that's when your 7-day free trial starts. You're not charged until day 8.

can i cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel during your trial and you won't be charged anything. Cancel any time after and you won't be billed for the next month.

is what i type private?

This isn't a public feed or a community forum — you're not posting for anyone else to see or respond to. Full details on how your responses are handled are in our terms, which you'll agree to when you create an account.

what if i only use it sometimes?

That's normal. This isn't about perfect daily use — it's here for the moments you actually need it, whenever those come up.
